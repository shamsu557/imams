About the Company

Built on Trust,
Driven by Purpose

THE IMAM'S SERVICES NIG LTD is a duly registered Nigerian private company incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) 2020 in April 2025. We are headquartered in Kano, Nigeria, and provide professional services across a broad spectrum of sectors.

Our commitment to quality, transparency, and timely delivery has earned us the trust of clients across Northern Nigeria. We are a company that holds excellence not as a goal, but as a standard.
